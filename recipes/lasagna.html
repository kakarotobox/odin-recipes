Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe is the best one of the world, passed through many generations of the same lovely family from Italy.

You will impress the most picky guess you ever invite over dinner any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • 200gr de pasta.
  • 1/2 lb de carne molida.
  • 2 cds de aceite.

Steps

  1. Juntar la pasta con la carne en un molde.
  2. Entrar el molde en el horno.
  3. Sacar el molde del horno despues de 45 minutos.
