Lasagna

Description

This page will ilustrate on how a lasagna is done right. Including the correct steps and ingredients anyone should use when cooking this dish.

The lasagna is a very well know dish all around the world and the favorite for many people.

Ingredients

    Basic ingredients
    • Ricotta cheese
    • Parmesan cheese
    • Eggs
    • Tomato sauce
    • Tomato paste
    • Garlic
    • Onion
    • Olive oil
    • Salt & pepper
    Seasonings & Herbs
    • Italian seasoning (or a mix of basil, oregano, thyme)
    • Red pepper flakes (optional for a bit of heat)
    • Fresh basil (for garnish or added flavor)

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
    • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    • Sauté onion and garlic until soft.
    • Add ground beef/sausage and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
    • Stir in tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes.
    • Season with salt, pepper, Italian seasoning, and red pepper flakes (if using).
    • Simmer for at least 20–30 minutes to develop flavor.
  2. Prepare the Ricotta mixture
    • In a bowl, mix ricotta cheese, 1–2 eggs, Parmesan cheese, salt, and pepper.
    • Optionally, add chopped fresh basil or spinach for extra flavor.
  3. Pre-Cook the Lasagna Noodles (Skip if using oven-ready noodles)
    • Boil lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente.
      • Drain and lay them flat on a baking sheet or parchment paper to prevent sticking.
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
    • In a 9x13-inch baking dish, start layering:
    • Spread a thin layer of sauce on the bottom.
    • Place a layer of noodles over the sauce.
    • Spread ricotta mixture evenly over the noodles.
    • Sprinkle shredded mozzarella and some Parmesan.
    • Repeat layers (sauce → noodles → ricotta → cheese) until ingredients are used up.
    • Finish with a top layer of noodles, sauce, and cheese.
  5. Bake the Lasagna
    • Cover with foil (tent it slightly to avoid cheese sticking).
    • Bake for 25 minutes covered, then remove foil and bake for another 15–20 minutes until cheese is golden and bubbly.
    • Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before slicing.

  6. Serve & Enjoy!
    • Garnish with fresh basil or parsley.
    • Serve with garlic bread or a side salad.
